A Mighty Morphin Power Rangers Matching Game
Go Go Power Rangers! This matching game was created to relive the excitement of watching power rangers on my home television. Be sure to turn your volume up!
The application utilized basic Javascript, CSS, and HTML using Object Oriented Programming. Photoshop was used to cut out the villains seen in the game, while Apple's Final Cut Pro was used to mesh video cutscenes together.
