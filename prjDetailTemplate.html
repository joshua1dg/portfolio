A Morning Productivity Dashboard For The
Self-Employed And Focused
If you win the morning you win the day. I created this dashboard as a way to help myself and others start off their day with as little distractions as possible. Instead of waking up and grabbing your phone, you can wake up and see all the information you need to get started with your day - without getting distracted by anything else.
This application was created using React, Redux, and Sass in the front end, PHP as the backend, and MySql for the database. Materialize was utilized for the login forms and credentials information.
